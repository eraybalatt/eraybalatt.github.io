About

Eray Balat

I'm Eray Balat, a Creative AI Producer and filmmaker known as documenteray (sometimes ÆRAY). I treat generative AI as a camera, an edit suite and a scoring stage, directing music videos, documentaries, product films and short films for brands and artists worldwide.

Cinema graduate from Erciyes University. I build custom AI pipelines (Nano Banana, Kling, Seedance, Flora, Suno) finished in Premiere Pro and After Effects. My signature: deep, cinematic frames with a glowing blue & citrine-gold palette.

  • 🎬Producer @ CITRINE — animated documentaries (Charlie Kirk, 17K views)

  • 💼“Dark-luxury” AI commercials for Doqu Home & Bellona (Balp Dijital)
  • 🏆“Boomerang” — Honorable Mention, Student Video Contest 2026
