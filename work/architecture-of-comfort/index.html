the challenge

comfort is hard to fake.

furniture has to read as soft, solid, and real. the eye catches a wrong seam or a flat shadow at once. every material has to feel right.

the brief was scale. a large catalog of furniture and home products, each shown across angles, rooms, and light.

a full photo shoot for all of it was not realistic. not on that budget, not on that timeline.

process

i treat rendering like a search.

i generate wide, then cut hard. thousands of options per product, chasing the right form, material, and light. only the frames that read as real survive.
