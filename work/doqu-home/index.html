the challenge

move at retail speed.

doqu home sells furniture. that means a fast calendar. new products, seasonal launches, and campaigns land every month.

traditional shoots could not keep that pace or budget. the brand needed motion that felt premium but shipped weekly.

they also wanted to grow on instagram. that called for a steady feed of scroll-stopping video, not one hero film a quarter.

process

one pipeline, built to repeat.

i built a single ai pipeline for the brand. it turns product references into finished motion in days, not weeks. the look stays consistent across every asset.
